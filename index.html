Noom Color Calculator

This is a calculator for Noom. It uses Noom's calorie density formula to assign food to the color categories green, yellow and red. Calorie density of a food is measured by dividing the calories in a serving by the weight (in grams) or the volume (in ml) of a serving.

Note: This calculator does not work for dairy products. In the Noom system dairy products are divided as follows:

  • Full fat dairy: Red
  • Low Fat dairy: Yellow
  • No Fat Dairy: Green
